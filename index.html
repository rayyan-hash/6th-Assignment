Wars

World War I
  • The United States entered World War I
  • Germany and Austria-Hungary entered World War I
  • The Axis powers, such as Germany, Austria-Hungary, and France, joined World War I
This conflict lasted from 1914 to 1918 and resulted in the rise of Nazi Germany. It was a major global conflict between the Allies and the Axis powers.

World War II
  • The United States entered World War II
  • Germany and Japan entered World War II
  • The Soviet Union entered World War II
  • The Axis powers, such as Italy, Austria-Hungary, and France, joined World War II
  • The United Nations entered World War II
  • The Cold War began
This conflict lasted from 1939 to 1945 and resulted in the end of World War I. It was a global conflict between the Allies and the Axis powers.

Post-World War II
This period began in the late 1940s and early 1950s. It saw the United States and its allies continue to fight for peace and security.
